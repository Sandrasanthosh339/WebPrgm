Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum on the southern bank of the river yamuna in the Indian city of Agra.It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shajahan.

The Taj Mahal was designated as a UNESCO world heritage site in 1983 for being "the jewel of muslim art in India and one of the universally admired masterpiece.
